About Us

We are a platform that connects people with venues for sports like padel, football, and basketball. Our website allows venue owners to list their spaces for rent, making it easy for anyone to browse, book, and enjoy playing at their favorite spots. Whether you're organizing a casual game or a competitive match, we help you find and reserve the perfect location with just a few clicks.
